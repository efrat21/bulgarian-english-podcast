# What We're Focused On

The app delivery base is in place, and the next live track is getting generated episodes into Podcast Addict on Android. The agreed first implementation path is a private local Wi-Fi RSS feed: convert audio to a podcast-friendly format, generate `podcast.xml`, and serve the feed plus episode files from the local machine over the LAN.
